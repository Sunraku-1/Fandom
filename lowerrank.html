Lower rank

The lower-ranked demons in "Demon Slayer" are the foot soldiers of Muzan Kibutsuji's dark army, driven by an insatiable hunger for human flesh and souls. Their purpose is to sow chaos, spread fear, and expand the influence of their malevolent master. Though lacking the power and cunning of their Upper Moon counterparts, these demons possess formidable strength, agility, and regenerative abilities, making them dangerous adversaries for the Demon Slayer Corps. Some demons exhibit unique abilities such as shape-shifting, manipulation of elemental forces, or unleashing deadly curses. Despite their individual skills, lower-ranked demons are ultimately united by their relentless pursuit of prey and their relentless loyalty to Muzan's dark cause.

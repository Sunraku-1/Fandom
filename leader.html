Leader

Kagaya Ubuyashiki serves as the leader of the Demon Slayer Corps with a deep sense of responsibility and wisdom. His purpose lies in upholding the legacy of the Corps, ensuring its members are well-trained and equipped to combat the demon threat, and guiding them with compassion and integrity. Kagaya's leadership embodies honor, sacrifice, and a commitment to protecting humanity from demonic forces.

On the other hand,Muzan Kibutsuji, the primary antagonist, assumes leadership over demons with a starkly contrasting motive. His purpose revolves around domination, power, and perpetuating his existence as the ultimate demon. Muzan seeks to expand his influence, creating more demons to serve his insatiable thirst for control and immortality.His leadership is characterized by manipulation, cruelty, and a relentless pursuit of his own selfish desires, posing a formidable challenge to the Demon Slayer Corps.

Upper Moon

The Upper Moons in "Demon Slayer" are elite demons, each possessing tremendous power and serving directly under Muzan Kibutsuji, the leader of all demons. Their mission is to carry out Muzan's orders and oversee various aspects of his grand design, including commanding lower-ranked demons, executing strategic plans, and eliminating threats to his reign.

Each Upper Moon possesses unique abilities and strengths, making them formidable adversaries for the Demon Slayer Corps. From the formidable combat prowess of Akaza, the Upper Moon Three, to the intricate manipulation of Doma, the Upper Moon Two, their powers range widely, but all share a single goal: to ensure the dominance of demonkind and the supremacy of their master, Muzan.

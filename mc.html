Main character

This is the main character in the anime titled Demon Slayer. First, Tanjiro is the one who uses the sun breathing technique and wants to help his sister become a normal girl again. With the help of his companions, they want to save people's lives from demons because demons kill people mercilessly, and what they want is to end the evil of demons.

Tanjiro Kamado, a determined and compassionate young man, leads his companions Zenitsu Agatsuma, a timid yet skilled swordsman, and Inosuke Hashibira, a wild and brash fighter, on their perilous journey as demon slayers. Alongside them is Nezuko Kamado, Tanjiro's sister who has been transformed into a demon but retains her humanity. Together, they seek to avenge their family and rid the world of demons, while Tanjiro holds onto the hope of finding a cure for Nezuko's condition. Their bond grows stronger with each battle they face, their resolve unwavering as they confront both the horrors of the demon world and the challenges within themselves.
